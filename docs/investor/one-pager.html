The Problem

Nigerian online commerce is already large — $9.35B in 2025 — but trust is the bottleneck. Buyers fear fake sellers and advance-payment scams; sellers waste time on unserious "DM for price" buyers. Most transactions migrate to WhatsApp the moment serious negotiation starts, taking the dispute trail with them.

The Solution

ShowMePrice builds trust into the transaction flow, not as a badge. Real prices required. Phone-verified buyers. In-app messaging by default (creates dispute trail). Pro buyers unlock direct WhatsApp/phone — a paid "serious buyer" signal sellers can see. Two-tier escrow protects both mid-value (₦25K+, ₦500 flat) and high-value (₦50K+, 1.5% + ₦100) transactions; buyer-gated, never requires seller subscription. Admin moderation with reports and blocks. Phase E ships the foundation; later phases add seller tools and logistics-backed fulfillment.

Market

Nigerian e-commerce: $9.35B (2025) → $18.68B (2031), ~12% CAGR. Social commerce: $2.04B → $3.96B by 2030. Mobile-first, 80%+ of online orders. Diaspora remittances: $24B+/year — an underserved high-LTV buyer segment.

Wedge

Lagos-first. Phones, laptops, electronics, appliances, generators — categories where price transparency, seller credibility, and payment safety matter most. 50–100 verified sellers before aggressive buyer acquisition. We compete on trust density, not listing volume.

Business Model

  • Pro Buyer subscription — ₦5,000/mo · ₦45,000/yr — contact reveal, SMS alerts, Pro badge, 33% escrow discount
  • Credit packs — ₦500–₦7,000 — pay-per-use contact reveal; 96% margin; expected to lead by transaction count in Y1
  • Diaspora Buyer — $15/mo · $150/yr — USD payment, delivery coordination, ~₦80K LTV per subscriber
  • Two-tier Escrow — Lite ₦500 flat (₦25K–₦50K) + Standard 1.5%+₦100 (₦50K+); buyer-gated, free sellers receive escrow orders
  • Pro Seller (Phase F) — ₦7,500/mo — 50 listings, boosts, analytics with measurable conversion lift
  • Boosts + Featured Seller (Phase F) — ₦2,500–₦30K — trust-gated, never overrides reply-rate ranking
  • Institution + Logistics (Phase G+) — custom plans, delivery coordination, fulfillment bundles
